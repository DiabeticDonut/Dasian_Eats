Chilli Paneer

Chilli Paneer is a popular Indian starter made by seasoning fried Indian cottage cheese aka paneer in chilli sauce.

Prep Cook Total Serves
7 mins 18 mins 25 mins 4

Ingredients

  • 200 grams Paneer(Cubed to 3/4 by 3/4 inch pieces)
  • 1/4 teaspoon red chilli powder (kashmiri or less spicy kind)(optional)
  • 3 tablespoons corn flour (corn starch)
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour (optional)
  • 2 pinches black pepper (crushed or ground)
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt to taste
  • Water (as needed)
  • 3 to 4 tablespoon oil (as needed for shallow frying)

For the sauce

  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons red chilli sauce
  • 2 tablespoons tomato ketchup
  • 1/2 to 1 teaspoon vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (adjust to taste)

Other ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon garlic (finely chopped)
  • 2 tablespoon spring onions (greens chopped)
  • 2 tablespoon spring onion (whites chopped)
  • 1/2 cup bell bepper (cubed)
  • 1 medium onion (cubed, layers seperated)
  • 1 green chillies (slit and deseeded)
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper powder (crushed black pepper)
  • 1 teaspoon corn flour (2 tsps for more gravy)
  • 1/2 cup water (1 cup for more gravy) (adjuat as needed)

Instructions

  1. For dry chilli paneer: Stir in 1 tsp corn flour to a bowl and mix with half cup water. Stir well and set aside.
  2. Remove the oil from the pan retaining 1 tbsp. Fry one tablespoon finely chopped garlic until it smells good.
  3. Then add in 2 tablespoons of spring onion whites and 1 medium onion cubed and layers separated.
  4. Also add in 1 slit green chili and half cup diced bell pepper. Saute on a high flame for 1 to 2 mins.
  5. Stir in soya sauce, red chilli sauce, tomato ketchup, sugar, vinegar and red chilli powder or paste.
  6. Mix the corn flour mixture and pour it to the pan. Cook till the sauce thickens.
  7. Check the taste of the sauce.
  8. If you want to thin down the suace, just add more water to get the desired consistency.
  9. Add fried paneer cubes, crushed pepper to the chilli sauce and mix.
  10. Add the spring onion greens.
  11. Serve paneer chilli hot as a appetizer. You can also serve this with noodles or fried rice.
