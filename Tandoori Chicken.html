Tandoori Chicken

Tandoori chicken or Tandoori Murgh - an iconic restaurant style smoky grilled chicken dish, that is authentic, easy and best! Perfect for party or gatherings.

Prep Cook Marination time Total Serves
5 mins 30 mins 9 hrs 9 hrs 35 mins 6

Ingredients

  • 2.5 Lbs Chicken Legs de-skinned and trimmed fat, cut into serving pieces

For the first marinade:

  • 1 tablespoon Ginger Garlic Paste
  • 2-3 teaspoon Kashmiri Chili Powde
  • 1 tablespoon Lemon Juice
  • Salt To Taste

For the second marinade:

  • 300 grams Greek Yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon Ginger Garlic Paste
  • 2-3 teaspoons Kashmiri Red Chili Powder
  • 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder
  • 1 teaspoon Garam Masala Powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon Black Salt or regular salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon Black Pepper Powder
  • 1 teaspoon Cumin Powder
  • Â½ cup Mustard Oil
  • Red Food Colour (optional)

Instructions

Preparation of Tandoori Chicken Marinade

  1. Clean, wash and make cuts on the chicken using a sharp knife. This will help the marinade to penetrate well in the chicken
  2. In a large bowl, mix all the ingredients of first marinade.
  3. Add chicken and mix it very well in the marinade.
  4. Cover the bowl with a plastic cover and leave it for 45 minutes - 1 hour under refrigeration.
  5. In another large bowl, add all the ingredients of second marinade and mix it very well.
  6. After the first marination is done, transfer the chicken in this second marinade. Cover the bowl with a cling film cover and refrigerate for 7-8 hours or overnight.

Making the Tandoori Chicken

  1. Take the chicken out 30-45 minutes prior to cooking.
  2. Pre-heat the oven at 425 degrees fahrenheit. Remove the chicken from the marinade and place it on a baking tray or a rack.
  3. Grill the chicken for 25 minutes and then broil it for 5 minutes or till done.
  4. Sprinkle some chaat masala over it, and serve along with mint chutney, lemon wedges and onion rings. Enjoy it as is or pair it with some naan bread or saffron rice.
